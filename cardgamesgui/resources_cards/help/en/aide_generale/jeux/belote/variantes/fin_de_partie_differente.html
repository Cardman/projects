Here is the variants at end of deal:

 The temporary full score of the taker is the definitive full score of the taker.

Examples for a different end of deal:

  1. If the taker''s team scores 75 points in the team''s tricks,
      if the taker''s team wins the last trick,
      if the defence owns the declaring belote rebelote,
      then the taker has a temporary score of 85 points, that is under 91 points.
      So the taker looses the deal and scores 85 points and the taker''s partner also, and each defender scores 97 points.
  2. If the taker''s team scores 75 points in the team''s tricks,
      if the taker''s team wins the last trick,
      then the taker has a temporary score of 85 points, that is over 81 points.
      So the taker wins the deal and scores 85 points and the taker''s partner also, and each defender scores 77 points.
  3. If the taker''s team wins all tricks,
      if the taker''s team owns the declaring belote-rebelote,
      then the taker has a temporary score of 182 points, that is over 81 points.
      So the taker wins the deal and scores 282 points and the taker''s partner also, and each defender scores zero points.
