The game is made up by:

  1. 12 characters.
    1. 4 kings.
    2. 4 queens.
    3. 4 jacks.
  2. 36 cards numbered from 2 to 10.
  3. 4 aces.

As many card games, there are 4 suits: heart, spade, diamond, club; for the characters and numbered cards.
For each suit, each type of cards is single.(type of cards: king, queen...)
