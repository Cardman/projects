The base declaring are handful and slam.

For slam, if the taker owns besides the Excuse, the taker must play it at the last trick,
because the taker has no partner and the Excuse, played before the last trick, does not allow to lead.

A handful is a hand containing only trump cards (The Excuse can be a trump card).
Adjectives simple, double et triple qualify the number of present trump cards in the handful.

The minimal numbers of trump cards (Excuse included) for declaring a simple handful,
a double handful and a triple handful are respectively 10, 13 and 15.

The Excuse can belong to a handful only if the full number of trump cards (Excuse included) is the matching minimal number.

Examples:

  1. If a player owns 9 trump cards and the Excuse,
      then for declaring the simple handful, the declarer must add the Excuse in the handful.
  2. If a player owns 10 trump cards and the Excuse,
      then for declaring the simple handful, the declarer must not add the Excuse in the handful.
  3. If a player owns 13 trump cards and the Excuse,
      then the player has not to declare a double handful.

Declaring handfuls or miseres is done at the beginning of deal by playing the first card.
Handfuls must be shown, while declaring handfuls or else, there may be false declaring.
