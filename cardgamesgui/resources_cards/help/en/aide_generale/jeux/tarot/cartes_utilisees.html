The game is made up by:

  1. 21 trumps numbered from 1 to 21.
  2. 56 cards of non trump suit.
    1. 16 characters.
      1. 4 kings.
      2. 4 queens.
      3. 4 knights.
      4. 4 jacks.
    2. 40 low cards numbered from 1 to 10.
  3. 1 card called "Excuse".Sometimes, this card is regarded as a trump card, an other time no.

As many card games, there are 4 suits: heart, spade, diamond, club; for kings, queens, knights, jacks and low cards.
For each suit, each type of cards is single.(type of cards: king, queen...)
