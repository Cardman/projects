A slam is a declaring when, the declarer (the player who has declared it) must win all tricks, that is says win the cards of the opponents of the player.
For declaring it, the player must declare a bid other than "Pass".

  1. If the taker has declared "Take" or "Guard":
      The dog is shown to all players.
      The taker takes the "dog" cards.
      For having the same number of cards than the other players, the taker discards from the hand the same number of cards as the dog contained.

      The taker cannot discard trump cards(The Excuse is, here, a trump card.) or kings.
      For trump cards, if the taker has a sum of number of trump cards (The Excuse is, here, a trump card.) and number of kings(trump cards + kings)greater than the number of cards per player,
      then the taker shows the discarded trump cards to the defense,
      in this case, the taker can now discard them from the hand.

      The discarded cards from the hand are hidden for the defense.
      After discarding cards to the dog, the taker can declare a slam.
  2. If the taker has declared "Guard without" or "Guard against":
      The dog stays hidden.
      If the taker has declared "Guard without", then the dog belongs to the taker.
      If the taker has declared "Guard against", then the dog belongs to the defense.
      The taker can declare a slam.
