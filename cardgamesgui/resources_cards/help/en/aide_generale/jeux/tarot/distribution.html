The player who deals cards is the dealer.
From a deal to an other, the dealer changes,
that is say the next dealer is the player at the right of the previous dealer,
and the game is mixed and cut at each time.

Each player owns 18 cards and 6 cards make up a deck (called dog),
the cards are dealt 3 by 3.

The cards, hidden side, which make up the dog,
are dealt card by card at any time,
except for the first round and for the last round.
The dealer deals in the counter clock wise.

After all cards are dealt,
if a player owns only the Small as trump card
(It is said the Small is dried. The Excuse is, here, a trump card.) in the hand of the player,
then the deal is cancelled. The cards are redealt, in this case.
