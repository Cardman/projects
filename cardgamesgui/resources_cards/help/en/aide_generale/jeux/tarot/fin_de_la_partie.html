1. Value of cards

    1. Trump 21, Small and Excuse: 4,5 points each one (these are the 3 Oudlers)
    2. Kings: 4,5 points each one
    3. Queens: 3,5 points each one
    4. Knights: 2,5 points each one
    5. Jacks: 1,5 points each one
    6. Low cards and trump cards from 2 to 20: 0,5 point each one
  2. Calculation of scores

     Nb means "Number".
     +- means "Plus or minus".
     pts means "points".
     "Plus or minus 25" (+-25) means:
      if the taker succeeds the taker''s bid, it is worth "plus 25",
      if the taker looses, it is worth "minus 25".

    1. Ace to end:
      • 10 pts, if the team of the taker wins the Small in the last trick.
      • -10 pts, if the defense wins the Small in the last trick.
      • 0 pts, else.
    2. Handfuls:
      1. Simple: 20 pts, if the declarer wins the deal; -20 pts, else.
      2. Double: 30 pts, if the declarer wins the deal; -30 pts, else.
      3. Triple: 40 pts, if the declarer wins the deal; -40 pts, else.
    3. chelem:
      1. successful declared: 400 pts.
      2. successful not declared: 200 pts.
      3. failed declared: -200 pts.
      4. failed not declared: 0 pts.
    4. Rate of bid:
      1. Take: 1.
      2. Guard: 2.
      3. Guard without: 4.
      4. Guard against: 6.
  3. Success of a bid:

    • 56 points needed without oudler.
    • 51 points needed with 1 oudler won in the tricks of the taker.
    • 41 points needed with 2 oudlers won in the tricks of the taker.
    • 36 points needed with 3 oudlers won in the tricks of the taker.

      Amount of points for a deal: 91 points.
      Difference = Nb pts scored by the taker - Nb pts needed for winning the deal, for the taker
      Score of the taker without declaring points = +-25pts + Difference + Ace to end
      Declaring = Handfuls + Slam
      Full score of a defender = - (Score of the taker without declaring points x Rate of bid + Declaring taker - Declaring defense)
      Full score of the taker = -3 x Full score total of a defender

Examples:

  1. If the taker scores 43 points with 2 Oudlers in the tricks of the taker, by declaring "Take", neither declaring handful for all players, nor slam,
      if no player has played the Small at the last trick,
      then the taker wins the deal with 2 points and scores ((25+(43-41)+0)x1+0+0)x3=81 points, and each defender scores ((-25-(43-41)+0)x1+0+0)X1=-27 points.
  2. If the taker scores 43 points with 1 Oudler in the tricks of the taker, by declaring "Take", neither declaring handful for all players, nor slam,
      if no player has played the Small at the last trick,
      then the taker looses the deal with 8 points and scores ((-25+(43-51)+0)x1+0+0)x3=-99 points, and each defender scores ((25-(43-51)+0)x1+0+0)X1=33 points.
  3. If the taker scores 60 points avec 2 Oudlers in the tricks of the taker, by declaring "Guard", with a double handful, without handfuls for the defense, without slam,
      if the taker has played the Small at the last round without loosing it,
      then the taker wins the deal with 19 points and scores ((25+(60-41)+10)x2+30+0)x3=414 points, and each defender scores ((-25-(60-41)-10)x2-30+0)X1=-138 points.
