Here is changes with tarot played by 4 players:

  1. dealing

    1. If the taker plays against the 4 other players:
         each player owns 14 cards and 8 cards make up the dog,
         cards are dealt 2 by 2.
    2. Else:
         each player owns 15 cards and 3 cards make up the dog,
         cards are dealt 3 by 3.
  2. Repartition of teams

    1. First variant, the most played one:
         After all players having declared,
          the taker calls the king of a suit,
          if the taker has the 4 kings, the taker calls the queen of a suit,
          if the taker has the 4 queens and the 4 kings, the taker calls the knight of a suit,
          if the taker has the 4 knights, the 4 queens and the 4 kings, the taker calls the jack of a suit.
          In function by rules, the call can be done before or after the dog.
    2. Second variant:
         After all players having declared,
          the taker calls a character of a suit.
          In function by rules, the call can be done before or after the dog.
    3. Third variant:
         If there is no call, the taker plays against the other players.
  3. Declaring handfuls:

    1. simple handful: 8 trump cards (The Excuse is a trump card).
    2. double handful: 10 trump cards (The Excuse is a trump card).
    3. triple handful: 12 trump cards (The Excuse is a trump card).
  4. Calculation of scores at the end of deal:

      Amount of points for a deal: 91 points
      Difference = Nb pts scored by the taker - Nb pts needed for winning the deal, for the taker
      Score of the taker without declaring points = +-25pts + Difference + Ace to end
      Declaring = Handfuls + Slam + Possible Miseres
      Full score of a defender = - (Score of the taker without declaring points x Rate of bid + Declaring taker - Declaring defense)
      If the taker has a partner
       Full score of the taker = -2 x Full score total of a defender
      Else
       Full score of the taker = -4 x Full score total of a defender
      Full score of the possible called player = - Full score total of a defender

     If the taker score a not integer points,
      then the difference is rounded to the greater integer.

 Examples:

  1. If the taker scores 43,5 points with 2 Oudlers in the tricks, by declaring "Take", without declaring handfuls for all players, without slam,
      if the taker has a partner,
      if none of 5 players has played the Small at the last trick,
      then the taker wins the deal by 3 points and scores ((25+(44-41)+0)x1+0+0+0)x2=56 points,
       the called player scores (25+(44-41)+0)x1+0+0+0=28 points,
       each defender scores ((-25-(44-41)+0)x1+0+0+0)X1=-28 points.
  2. If the taker scores 43,5 points with 1 Oudler in the tricks, by declaring "Take", without declaring handfuls for all players, without slam,
      if none of 5 players has played the Small at the last trick,
      if the taker has no partner,
      then the taker looses the deal by 8 points and scores ((-25+(43-51)+0)x1+0+0+0)x4=-132 points, and each defender scores ((25-(43-51)+0)x1+0+0+0)X1=33 points.
