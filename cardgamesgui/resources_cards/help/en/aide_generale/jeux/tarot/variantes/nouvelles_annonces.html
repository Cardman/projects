The declaring miseres are:
  • Misere of trump: no trump card(The Excuse is a trump card here).
  • Misere of points: neither Oudler nor characters.
  • Misere of low cards: no low card from ace to ten.
  • Misere of suit: only trump cards and possibly the Excuse.
  • Misere of characters: no characters card.

Declaring misere is done as the same time than declaring handfuls, before playing the first card.

Their respective values are: 10, 10, 20, 30, 5.

At the end of deal,
  • If the taker declares miseres, then the declaring miseres points are added to declaring points of the taker.
  • If the possible partner of the taker declares miseres, then the declaring miseres points are added to declaring points of the taker.
  • If a defender declares miseres, then the declaring miseres points are remove from declaring points of the taker.

Example: (at 4 players classic)

 If the taker scores 43 points with 2 Oudlers in the tricks, by declaring "Take", without declaring handfuls for the 4 players, without slam,
  by with a misere of trump declared by a defender,
  if none of 4 players has played the Small at the last trick,
  then the taker wins the deal by 2 points and scores ((25+(43-41)+0)x1+0+0-10)x3=51 points, and each defender scores ((-25-(43-41)+0)x1+0+0+10)X1=-17 points.
