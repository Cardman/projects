The menu "File" owns four sub menus which are:
  1. Load a deal (shortcut: CTRL + C), accessible
    1. while no deal is played
    2. while a deal is played and put in pause
    3. while a deal is played and the user must choose at least one card
  2. Save a deal (shortcut: CTRL + S), accessible
    1. while a deal is played and put in pause
    2. while a deal is played and the user must choose at least one card
  3. Change game (shortcut: CTRL + J), accessible
    1. while a deal is played and put in pause and the deal has not just been saved
    2. while a deal is played and the user must choose at least one card
    3. while no deal is played and the buttons of the window have not a name of a card game
  4. Quit (shortcut: CTRL + W), always accessible
