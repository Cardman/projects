The menu "Parameters" owns eight sub menus which are:
  1. Belote (shortcut: MAJ + B), always accessible
  2. Tarot (shortcut: MAJ + T), always accessible
  3. Players (shortcut: CTRL + ALT + J), always accessible
  4. Launching option (shortcut: CTRL + O), always accessible
  5. Timing for playing (shortcut: F4), always accessible
  6. Interacting with cards (shortcut: F5), always accessible
  7. Language
  8. Displaying
