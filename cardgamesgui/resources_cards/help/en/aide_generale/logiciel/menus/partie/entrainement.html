The subs menu "Training" contains the sub menus about games for training.

There are three training modes at tarot:
  1. Haunt the Small
  2. Save the Small
  3. Lead the Small at the last trick

This is developped by editing random numbers.

A random number of trump cards is edited following a binomial conditionnal law;
for keeping the events with the same probability.
Intervals of numbers of trump cards to be edited are the following one: (The Small does not belong to the randomly edited trump cards.)

  1. At three players,
    1. Haunt the Small:
       The minimum is worth fifteen and the maximum is worth twenty one.
    2. Save the Small:
       The minimum is worth one and the maximum is worth five.
    3. Lead the Small at the last trick:
       The minimum is worth fourteen and the maximum is worth twenty one.
  2. At four players,
    1. Haunt the Small:
       The minimum is worth thirteen and the maximum is worth eighteen.
    2. Save the Small:
       The minimum is worth one and the maximum is worth four.
    3. Lead the Small at the last trick:
       The minimum is worth twelve and the maximum is worth seventeen.
  3. At five players, (with call)
    1. Haunt the Small:
       The minimum is worth ten and the maximum is worth fifteen.
    2. Save the Small:
       The minimum is worth one and the maximum is worth three.
    3. Lead the Small at the last trick:
       The minimum is worth nine and the maximum is worth fourteen.
  4. At five players, (without call)
    1. Haunt the Small:
       The minimum is worth ten and the maximum is worth fourteen.
    2. Save the Small:
       The minimum is worth one and the maximum is worth two.
    3. Lead the Small at the last trick:
       The minimum is worth nine and the maximum is worth thirteen.
  5. At six players
    1. Haunt the Small:
       The minimum is worth nine and the maximum is worth twelve.
    2. Save the Small:
       The minimum is worth one and the maximum is worth three.
    3. Lead the Small at the last trick:
       The minimum is worth eight and the maximum is worth eleven.

In all cases, a deal checking the above conditions, is played.
