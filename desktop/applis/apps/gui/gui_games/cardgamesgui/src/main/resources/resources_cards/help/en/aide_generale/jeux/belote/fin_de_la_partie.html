1. Value of cards
    1. trump:
      1. Jack: 20 points.
      2. 9: 14 points.
      3. Ace: 11 points.
      4. 10: 10 points.
      5. King: 4 points.
      6. Queen: 3 points.
      7. 8 and 7: 0 point.
    2. suit:
      1. Ace: 11 points.
      2. 10: 10 points.
      3. King: 4 points.
      4. Queen: 3 points.
      5. Jack: 2 points.
      6. 9, 8 et 7: 0 point.
  2. Calculation of scores

     Nb means "Nombre".
     pts means "points".

    1. Ten of last trick, (Win the last trick): 10 points.
    2. Belote rebelote: 20 points.
    3. chelem (For the team of the taker, if the team wins all tricks): 100 points.
  3. Success of bid:

      Full points for a deal: 162 points.
      Score of the taker without declaring points = Nb pts scored by the taker and the taker''s partner + Ten of last trick if the taker''s team wins the last trick.
      Declaring = Belote rebelote
      Temporary full score of the taker = Declaring of the taker''s team + Score of the taker without declaring point
      If the belote rebelote has been declared during the deal:
       If the full score of the taker is lower than 91 points,
        then the full score is set to zero (The taker looses.).
       Else the temporary full score of the taker becomes definitive.
      Else
       If the full score of the taker is lower than 81 points,
        then the full score is set to zero (The taker looses.).
       Else the temporary full score of the taker becomes definitive.
      Full score of defender = 162 - definitive full score of the taker
      If the taker looses and if the belote rebelote has been declared during the deal:
       Then the full score of a defender is 182.
      The taker''s partner and the taker win the same points.
      If all tricks are won by the taker''s team,
       Then, the chelem points are added to full score of each player of the taker''s team.

Examples:
  1. If the taker''s team scores 75 points in one''s tricks,
      if the taker''s team win the last trick,
      if the defense owns the declaring belote rebelote,
      then the taker has a temporary score of 85 points, that is under 91 points.
      So the taker looses the deal and score zero points and one''s partner also, and each defender scores 182 points.
  2. If the taker''s team scores 75 points in one''s tricks,
      if the taker''s team win the last trick,
      then the taker has a temporary score of 85 points, that is over 81 points.
      So the taker wins the deal and scores 85 points and one''s partner also, and each defender scores 77 points.
  3. If the taker''s team wins all tricks,
      if the taker''s team owns the declaring belote rebelote,
      then the taker has a temporary score of 182 points, that is over 81 points.
      So the taker wins the deal and scores 282 points and one''s partner also, and each defender scores zero points.
