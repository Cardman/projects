As for all card games, there are variants.

Here is some variants:

  1. The rules about playing trump cards can be changed:
    1. The player must undertrump (Play a trump card lower) on a player of the opposite team (current winner of the trick) if possible, if the dominant suit of a trick is not the trump suit.
    2. The player must undertrump and overtrump (Play a trump card greater) on the partner (current winner of the trick) if possible, if the dominant suit of a trick is not the trump suit.
    3. The player must overtrump on the partner (current winner of the trick) if possible, but not undertrump, if the dominant suit of a trick is not the trump suit.
  2. Play with bids "no trump" and "all trump".

  3. Different end of deal.

  4. Cards can be dealt in the clock wise.

  5. Cards may not be mixed.

  6. Cards may be mixed once only.

The variants numbered 2 and 3 are explained.
