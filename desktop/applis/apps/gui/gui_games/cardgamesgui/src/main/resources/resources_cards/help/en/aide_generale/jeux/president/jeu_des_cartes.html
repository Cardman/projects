President is played by groups of cards of same strength.
A trick is a set of cards won by a player having played the strongest group of cards which is played last.
If the player having won a trick has still cards, this player begins the next trick.(Example: the player having played a 2 begins the next trick.).
If the player having won a trick has finished, the next player who has cards begins the next trick.

The player who starts a trick is a starter.
The player who has played the latest and strongest group of cards of a trick is the winner of trick, this player wins the trick.

  1. The first group of cards of a trick
    The player at the right of the dealer begins to play. This player can play any group of cards of same strength.
  2. Rules
    1. The player can pass or play stronger cards over the last played cards. Cards of a group must be of same strength.
    2. Besides, for playing, the player must use the same count of cards as the count of cards played by the stater.
    3. A player who has passed cannot play while the current trick.
    4. If, at the most, one player has not passed, the trick ends. The leader become:
      1. the player who has played the last group of cards, if this player has cards.
      2. else the next player, who has cards, after the player who has played the last group of cards.
