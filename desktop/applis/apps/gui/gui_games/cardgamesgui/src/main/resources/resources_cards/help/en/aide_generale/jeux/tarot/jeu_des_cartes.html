The tarot is played card by card.
A trick is a set of cards won by a player having played the strongest card. The player having won a trick can start the next trick.(Example: the player having played the trump 21 can start the next trick.).

The player who starts the trick is called as starter.
The player who has played the strongest card of a trick is called as the winner of trick, the player wins the trick.

The dominant suit is the suit of the first played card in the trick if the card is not the Excuse; the suit of the second card else.
A trick is cut if there is at least one trump card (Excuse excluded).

  1. Playing the first card

    The player at the right of dealer begins playing a card of the choice of the player, once the taker has made up the dog.
    In case of slam, this is the taker who begins playing.
  2. Rules

    The Excuse can be played at any trick.

    1. If the first card is the Excuse,
      then the next player can play any card.
    2. If the dominant suit is the trump suit (The Excuse, here, is not a trump card.),
      1. If the player does not own any trump card
        the player can play any card.
      2. If the player can play a greater trump card,
        then the player must play a greater trump card.
      3. else, the player must play any trump card,

    3. If the dominant suit is not the trump suit,
      1. If the player owns cards of the suit of the first card,
        then the player must follow any card of the dominant suit.
      2. Else if the player does not own any trump card,
        the player can play any card.
      3. Else if no trump card is played,
        the player must play any trump card, the player trumps.
      4. Else if the player can play a greater card than the current strongest played trump card,
        then the player must play a greater trump card than the previous trump cards, the player owertrumps.
      5. else the player must play any trump card, the player undertrumps.


Before the last trick, the Excuse does not let win a trick. The Excuse is untakable.
The Excuse is substituted by a low card or a trump card numbered from 2 to 20.

At the last trick, if all previous tricks are not won by the team of the player owing the Excuse (or by the taker),
then the Excuse is takable and belongs to the team of the player who wins the last trick.

If a player with possibly partners wins tricks from the 1st to the before last trick and played the Excuse at the last trick,
then the player achieves a slam even not declared.
The possible partner(s) of a player, declaring a slam, have/has not to play the Excuse at the last trick.
