Here is the difference with playing by teams (Taker + possible called player versus Defense):

Possible dealing are the following one:
  • 18 cards per player and 6 at the dog; cards are dealt 3 by 3; for 4 players.
  • 24 cards per player and 6 at the dog; cards are dealt 3 by 3; for 3 players.
  • 15 cards per player and 3 at the dog; cards are dealt 3 by 3; for 5 players.
  • 14 cards per player and 8 at the dog; cards are dealt 2 by 2; for 5 players.
  • 12 cards per player and 6 at the dog; cards are dealt 2 by 2; for 6 players.

Playing one for one can be done after all players have declared "pass".

In this case, the dog is hidden.

The goal is to get the most cards as possible.

Else the deal progresses like game by teams (Same declaring and same rules for playing cards.).

 Limit values to be achieved for a player are the following one:

  • 56 points without Oulder.
  • 51 points with 1 Oulder in the tricks of the player.
  • 41 points with 2 Oulders in the tricks of the player.
  • 36 points with 3 Oulders in the tricks of the player.

 At the end of deal, for sorting players, one processes in the following way:


Difference points of a player = number of scored points in the tricks - limit value for the player.

Players are sorted by their difference points, the player having the greatest difference is the best player.

If two players have same difference points and have won tricks:
 Then the players are sorted by number of Oudlers in the tricks, the number of Oudlers must be the greatest as possible for winning.

 If besides, the number of Oudlers in the tricks of the two players is the same, then:
  1. If this number is 0:
       Numbers of characters in the tricks of the two players is compared, the number of characters in the tricks of a player must be the greatest as possible for winning.
       If besides, the number of characters in the tricks of the two players is the same, then:
        Won characters by the two players are compared, by firstly comparing the greatest characters.
        Comparison is stopped, if there is a difference of strength of card.
        If the characters have the same strength two by two for the two players,
         the first player who has won a trick is before the other player.
  2. If this number is 1:
       Sorting on Oudlers is based in the following way from the most winning to the most loosing: Trump 21 - Excuse - Small.
       (Winning the Excuse is better than winning the Small,...)

If two players have same difference points but have not won any trick, there is dispute.

  • 3 players
      Rate is worth from the winner to the looser:
    • 1, 0, -1; if there is no dispute.
    • 2, -1, -1; if there is dispute.
  • 4 players
      Rate is worth from the winner to the looser:
    • 2, 1, -1, -2; if there is no dispute.
    • 3, 1, -2, -2; if there is dispute between two players exactly.
    • 6, -2, -2, -2; if there is dispute between three players exactly.
  • 5 players
      Rate is worth from the winner to the looser:
    • 2, 1, 0, -1, -2; if there is no dispute.
    • 3, 1, 0, -2, -2; if there is dispute between two players exactly.
    • 6, 0, -2, -2, -2; if there is dispute between three players exactly.
    • 8, -2, -2, -2, -2; if there is dispute between four players exactly.
  • 6 players
      Rate is worth from the winner to the looser:
    • 3, 2, 1, -1, -2, -3; if there is no dispute.
    • 3, 2, 1, 0, -3, -3; if there is dispute between two players exactly.
    • 4, 2, 0, -2, -2, -2; if there is dispute between three players exactly.
    • 8, 0, -2, -2, -2, -2; if there is dispute between four players exactly.
    • 10, -2, -2, -2, -2, -2; if there is dispute between five players exactly.

A successful slam is by default 200 points. (The slam is regarded as not declared.)

Score of a player = 4 x (Rate x (25 + Maximal difference points for players rounded to the greater integer + number of points in the dog rounded to the greater integer) + (number of players - 1) x Declaring points of player - Declaring of each one of other players).

By teams of two players, rates are the following one:

  • 4 players
      Rate is worth from the winner team to the looser team:
    • 1, -1
  • 6 players
      Rate is worth from the winner team to the looser team:
    • 1, 0, -1; if there is no dispute.
    • 2, -1, -1; if there is dispute between two teams exactly.
