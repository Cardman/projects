Timing for playing shortcut F4
The action of the sub menu "Timing for playing" lets change
  1. delay between two cards consecutively played by the "robots".
  2. delay between two tricks or rounds consecutively played.
  3. delay between two bids consecutively played by the "robots".

This action shows a dialog box.

This dialog box lets fix delay between:
  1. Two consecutive cards.
  2. Two consecutive bids.
  3. Two consecutive tricks.

Besides, this dialog box lets
enable or disabled delay between two consecutive tricks,
without clicking buttons at bottom right of the window.
